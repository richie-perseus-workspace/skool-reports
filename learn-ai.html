Observations

  • The community has a large volume of 'I'm All In' and 'I've chosen my path' posts that generate almost no discussion or follow-up engagement, suggesting these onboarding prompts are creating activity noise without building real community momentum or moving members toward action.
  • There is a visible and unmet demand for builder-plus-sales partnerships, with at least six separate posts from builders seeking someone to handle client acquisition and several others from sales-oriented people seeking technical partners, yet no structured matchmaking or collaboration mechanism exists inside the community to capture this.
  • Members who share specific technical builds with real numbers and outcomes (posts 7, 13, 35, 77, 135, 174) consistently generate the most substantive engagement, while motivational and mindset posts generate reactions but little actionable conversation, pointing to a clear content format that outperforms everything else in this community.
