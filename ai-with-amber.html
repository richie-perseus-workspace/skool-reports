Observations

  • The weekly sales workshop and backend Q&A calls are chronically under-attended relative to community size, with one post noting only 5 people out of 655 members showed up, suggesting the live call format is not reaching most members and recordings or async alternatives would unlock significantly more value for the majority.
  • There is a visible split between members who are actively dialing and hitting real sales problems versus members who are still stuck on tech setup, and the two groups rarely get answered in the same thread, meaning content that bridges the gap between 'got the meeting' and 'built the system' is almost entirely missing.
  • Multiple members are openly looking for accountability partners, cold calling session buddies, and sales roleplay practice, pointing to a strong unmet demand for structured peer-to-peer practice that could be formalized into a recurring community event rather than left to scattered DM requests.
