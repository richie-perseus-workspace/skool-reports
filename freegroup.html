Observations

  • The vast majority of engagement is concentrated in Day 1 accountability posts, but there is almost no visible follow-through content from the same members on Days 2 through 7, which suggests either a significant drop-off in challenge completion or a lack of prompting to keep posting accountability updates publicly.
  • There is a recurring and unmet need for international members, specifically those from Pakistan, Nigeria, and India, who are hitting friction with tool access (GHL verification, payment methods, SIP trunking for non-US numbers) that the core curriculum does not address, and these members are posting questions that go unanswered.
  • Several members are actively looking to partner up by splitting sales and fulfillment roles, and others are posting hiring requests for outreach partners and editors, which signals an organic collaboration marketplace forming inside the community that has no dedicated space or structure to support it.
