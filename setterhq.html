Observations

  • The mock call and roleplay demand is enormous and completely fragmented. At least 15 separate posts are members posting individual Calendly links with no coordination, which means most of them are probably getting zero bookings. A single pinned weekly pairing thread or a dedicated channel would immediately make the community feel more alive and useful.
  • There is a visible gap between members who are consuming content and members who are actually on offers and making money. The wins being posted (post 55, 65, 147, 156) are almost entirely from the same one or two people, while the majority of the community is stuck pre-role. Content or programming that specifically bridges the gap from trained-but-unemployed to placed would address the most common pain point in the room.
  • Several members are from outside the US (UK, Australia, Argentina, Norway, Pakistan, Lithuania) and are asking geography-specific questions with no clear answers in the feed, suggesting an unmet need for content that directly addresses timezone, offer selection, and outreach strategy for non-US setters.
